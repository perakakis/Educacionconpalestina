First Day Commitment

A pledge for inclusive and engaging teaching practices

Our Commitments

  • I will learn and use every student's name within the first two weeks of class
  • I will clearly communicate course expectations and learning objectives on the first day
  • I will create an inclusive classroom environment where all students feel welcome
  • I will be available during posted office hours and respond to student emails within 48 hours
  • I will provide clear rubrics and timely feedback on assignments
  • I will acknowledge when I don't know something and work to find the answer
  • I will treat all students with respect and encourage their academic growth
  • I will continuously work to improve my teaching methods based on student feedback
